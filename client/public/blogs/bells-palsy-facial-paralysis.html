What is Bell’s Palsy?

Bell’s palsy is sudden weakness or paralysis of the facial muscles, usually on one side. It is often temporary, but early physiotherapy can speed up recovery and restore facial movement.

How Physiotherapy Helps

  • Facial muscle exercises
  • Massage and stimulation techniques
  • Education for eye and mouth care
  • Support for emotional well-being
