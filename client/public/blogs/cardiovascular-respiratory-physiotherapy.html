Cardiovascular and Respiratory Physiotherapy

By Dukhniwarn Physio | Updated January 2026

Cardiovascular and respiratory physiotherapy helps patients with heart and lung conditions improve their health, exercise tolerance, and quality of life.

Common Conditions Treated

  • Coronary artery disease
  • Heart failure
  • Post-heart surgery recovery
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
  • Asthma
  • Bronchiectasis
  • Pneumonia recovery

Symptoms

  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue
  • Chest pain or tightness
  • Reduced exercise capacity
  • Frequent respiratory infections

Physiotherapy Assessment

Assessment includes medical history, exercise tolerance tests, and breathing pattern evaluation. Individualized rehab plans are created for each patient.

Rehabilitation Strategies

  • Supervised exercise programs
  • Breathing exercises
  • Airway clearance techniques
  • Education on lifestyle modification
  • Energy conservation strategies
  • Monitoring and support

Benefits of Physiotherapy

  • Improved heart and lung function
  • Increased exercise tolerance
  • Reduced symptoms
  • Better quality of life

When to Seek Help

If you have a heart or lung condition, consult a physiotherapist for a personalized rehabilitation plan to improve your health and well-being.
