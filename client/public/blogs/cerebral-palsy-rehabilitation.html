What is Cerebral Palsy?

Cerebral palsy (CP) is a group of disorders affecting movement and posture, often caused by brain injury before or during birth. Physiotherapy is essential for improving mobility, independence, and quality of life for both children and adults with CP.

How Physiotherapy Helps

  • Strengthening and stretching exercises
  • Balance and coordination training
  • Gait and mobility training
  • Assistive device recommendations
  • Family education and support
