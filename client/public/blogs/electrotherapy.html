What is Electrotherapy?

Electrotherapy uses controlled electrical stimulation to relieve pain, reduce swelling, and promote muscle recovery. It is a safe, non-invasive treatment used for a variety of musculoskeletal and neurological conditions.

Benefits of Electrotherapy

  • Reduces pain and inflammation
  • Improves muscle strength and function
  • Speeds up recovery from injuries
  • Decreases swelling and edema
  • Enhances nerve function

How is Electrotherapy Performed?

Our physiotherapists place electrodes on the skin over the affected area. Gentle electrical impulses are delivered, which may cause a tingling sensation. The treatment is comfortable and tailored to your needs.

Is Electrotherapy Safe?

Electrotherapy is safe when administered by trained professionals. At Dukhniwaran Physiotherapy, we use modern equipment and follow strict safety protocols.

Who Can Benefit?

Electrotherapy is effective for people with chronic pain, muscle weakness, nerve injuries, and post-surgical recovery. We assess your condition to ensure this therapy is right for you.
