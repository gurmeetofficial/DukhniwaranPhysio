What is Frozen Shoulder?

Frozen shoulder (adhesive capsulitis) causes pain and stiffness in the shoulder joint, making movement difficult. Rotator cuff injuries affect the muscles and tendons that stabilize your shoulder.

Symptoms

  • Shoulder pain and stiffness
  • Limited range of motion
  • Weakness in the arm
  • Pain at night or with overhead activities

How Physiotherapy Helps

We use gentle mobilization, stretching, strengthening exercises, and pain-relieving modalities to restore shoulder function. Early intervention leads to better outcomes.
