Geriatric Physiotherapy

By Dukhniwarn Physio | Updated January 2026

Geriatric physiotherapy helps older adults maintain mobility, independence, and quality of life by addressing age-related changes and health conditions.

Common Conditions Treated

  • Arthritis
  • Osteoporosis
  • Balance and gait disorders
  • Post-fracture rehabilitation
  • Parkinsonâ€™s disease
  • Stroke recovery

Goals of Geriatric Physiotherapy

  • Maintain or improve mobility
  • Reduce risk of falls
  • Enhance strength and balance
  • Promote independence
  • Manage pain and chronic conditions

Rehabilitation Strategies

  • Balance and strength training
  • Mobility and flexibility exercises
  • Fall prevention education
  • Pain management techniques
  • Assistive device training

Benefits of Physiotherapy

  • Improved mobility and function
  • Greater independence
  • Reduced pain and risk of falls
  • Enhanced quality of life

When to Seek Help

If you or a loved one is experiencing mobility issues or recovering from illness or injury, consult a geriatric physiotherapist for a personalized plan.
