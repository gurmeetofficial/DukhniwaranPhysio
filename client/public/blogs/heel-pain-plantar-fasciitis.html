What is Plantar Fasciitis?

Plantar fasciitis is inflammation of the tissue on the bottom of your foot, causing heel pain, especially with the first steps in the morning. Other causes of heel pain include overuse, poor footwear, and injury.

Symptoms

  • Sharp heel pain, especially in the morning
  • Tenderness at the bottom of the foot
  • Pain after standing or walking

How Physiotherapy Helps

We use manual therapy, stretching, strengthening, footwear advice, and pain-relieving modalities to reduce symptoms and promote healing.
