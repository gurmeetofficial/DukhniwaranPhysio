IASTM Therapy: Instrument-Assisted Soft Tissue Mobilization

By Dr. Jasmeet Singh | January 2026 | Dukhniwaran Physiotherapy

What is IASTM?

Instrument-Assisted Soft Tissue Mobilization (IASTM) is a specialized technique that uses ergonomic tools to break down scar tissue, release fascial restrictions, and improve mobility. It is highly effective for treating chronic pain and soft tissue injuries.

Benefits of IASTM

  • Reduces pain and muscle tightness
  • Breaks down scar tissue and adhesions
  • Improves range of motion
  • Speeds up recovery from injuries
  • Enhances athletic performance

How is IASTM Performed?

Our physiotherapists use specially designed tools to gently massage and mobilize soft tissues. The treatment is tailored to your needs and may cause mild redness, which fades quickly.

Is IASTM Safe?

IASTM is safe when performed by trained professionals. At Dukhniwaran Physiotherapy, we use high-quality instruments and follow strict safety protocols.

Who Can Benefit?

IASTM is ideal for people with chronic pain, sports injuries, limited mobility, and post-surgical scars. We assess your condition to ensure this therapy is right for you.
