What is Osteoarthritis?

Osteoarthritis is a degenerative joint disease that affects the cartilage in your knees and hips, causing pain, stiffness, and reduced mobility. It is common with aging but can be managed effectively with physiotherapy.

Symptoms

  • Joint pain and stiffness
  • Swelling and tenderness
  • Difficulty walking or climbing stairs
  • Reduced range of motion

How Physiotherapy Helps

We design personalized exercise programs, use manual therapy, and teach joint protection strategies to reduce pain and improve function. Our goal is to help you stay active and independent.
