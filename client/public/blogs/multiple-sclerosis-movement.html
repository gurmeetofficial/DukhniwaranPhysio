What is Multiple Sclerosis?

Multiple sclerosis (MS) is a chronic neurological condition that affects the brain and spinal cord, leading to muscle weakness, balance problems, and fatigue. Physiotherapy can help manage symptoms and maintain function.

Symptoms

  • Muscle weakness and spasticity
  • Balance and coordination issues
  • Fatigue and walking difficulties
  • Numbness or tingling

How Physiotherapy Helps

  • Strengthening and stretching exercises
  • Balance and gait training
  • Fatigue management strategies
  • Education for independence and safety
