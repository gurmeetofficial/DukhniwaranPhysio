What is Parkinson’s Disease?

Parkinson’s disease is a progressive neurological disorder that affects movement, balance, and coordination. Physiotherapy can help manage symptoms, improve mobility, and maintain independence.

Symptoms

  • Tremors and stiffness
  • Slowed movement (bradykinesia)
  • Balance and walking difficulties
  • Postural instability

How Physiotherapy Helps

  • Balance and gait training
  • Strengthening and flexibility exercises
  • Strategies for safe movement and fall prevention
  • Education for patients and caregivers
