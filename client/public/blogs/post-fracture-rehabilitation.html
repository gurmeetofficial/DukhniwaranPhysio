Post-Fracture Rehabilitation and Joint Stiffness Recovery

By Dr. Jasmeet Singh | January 2026 | Dukhniwaran Physiotherapy

Why is Rehabilitation Important After a Fracture?

After a bone fracture, joints and muscles can become stiff and weak. Physiotherapy is essential to restore movement, strength, and function, and to prevent long-term complications.

How Physiotherapy Helps

  • Gentle mobilization and stretching
  • Strengthening exercises
  • Pain management and swelling reduction
  • Education for safe return to activity
