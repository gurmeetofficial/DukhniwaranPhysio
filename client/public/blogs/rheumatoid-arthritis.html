What is Rheumatoid Arthritis?

Rheumatoid arthritis (RA) is an autoimmune condition that causes chronic inflammation of the joints, leading to pain, swelling, and reduced mobility. Early intervention and physiotherapy can help manage symptoms and prevent joint damage.

Symptoms

  • Joint pain, swelling, and stiffness
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Reduced range of motion
  • Warmth and redness in affected joints

How Physiotherapy Helps

We use gentle exercises, joint protection techniques, pain-relieving modalities, and education to help you manage RA and maintain independence.
