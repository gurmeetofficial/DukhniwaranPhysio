Understanding Slipped Disc & PIVD

A slipped disc (also called PIVD or herniated disc) occurs when the soft cushion between your spinal bones bulges or ruptures, pressing on nerves and causing pain, numbness, or weakness. Chronic back pain can result from disc issues, poor posture, or injury.

Symptoms

  • Sharp or shooting pain in the back or legs
  • Numbness or tingling in the limbs
  • Muscle weakness
  • Difficulty standing or walking
