Why is Rehabilitation Important After Spinal Cord Injury?

Spinal cord injuries can cause paralysis, weakness, and loss of function. Early and specialized physiotherapy is crucial for maximizing recovery, independence, and quality of life.

How Physiotherapy Helps

  • Strengthening and mobility exercises
  • Balance and coordination training
  • Functional retraining for daily activities
  • Assistive device recommendations
  • Education and support for patients and families
