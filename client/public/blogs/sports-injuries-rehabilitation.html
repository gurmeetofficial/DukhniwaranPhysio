Sports Injuries Rehabilitation

By Dukhniwarn Physio | Updated January 2026

Sports injuries are common among athletes and active individuals. Physiotherapy helps in the recovery, prevention, and safe return to sports after injury.

Common Sports Injuries

  • Sprains and strains
  • Ligament injuries (e.g., ACL tear)
  • Muscle tears
  • Fractures
  • Dislocations
  • Tendinitis
  • Shin splints

Symptoms

  • Pain and swelling
  • Bruising
  • Limited movement
  • Instability
  • Weakness

Physiotherapy Assessment

Assessment includes injury evaluation, movement analysis, and functional testing. Treatment plans are tailored to the type and severity of injury.

Rehabilitation Strategies

  • Pain management (modalities, manual therapy)
  • Restoration of movement and strength
  • Sport-specific exercises
  • Balance and proprioception training
  • Gradual return-to-play protocols
  • Injury prevention education

Benefits of Physiotherapy

  • Faster recovery
  • Reduced risk of re-injury
  • Improved performance
  • Safe return to activity

When to Seek Help

If you experience pain, swelling, or loss of function after a sports injury, consult a physiotherapist for a comprehensive assessment and rehabilitation plan.
