Why is Rehabilitation Important After a Stroke?

Stroke can cause weakness, paralysis, and loss of function on one side of the body. Early and specialized physiotherapy is crucial for regaining movement, independence, and confidence.

How Physiotherapy Helps

  • Strengthening and mobility exercises
  • Balance and coordination training
  • Functional retraining for daily activities
  • Education and support for patients and families
