Understanding the Conditions

Tennis elbow and golfer’s elbow are overuse injuries causing pain on the outside or inside of the elbow. Carpal tunnel syndrome is caused by pressure on the median nerve in the wrist, leading to pain, numbness, and weakness in the hand.

Symptoms

  • Elbow or wrist pain
  • Weak grip strength
  • Numbness or tingling in the hand or fingers
  • Pain with repetitive movements

How Physiotherapy Helps

We use manual therapy, targeted exercises, ergonomic advice, and pain-relieving modalities to reduce symptoms and restore function.
