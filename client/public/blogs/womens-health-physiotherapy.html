Women’s Health Physiotherapy

By Dukhniwarn Physio | Updated January 2026

Women’s health physiotherapy addresses unique needs related to pregnancy, postpartum recovery, pelvic pain, and incontinence, supporting women’s well-being at every stage of life.

Common Conditions Treated

  • Pregnancy-related pain
  • Postpartum recovery
  • Pelvic floor dysfunction
  • Urinary incontinence
  • Pelvic pain
  • Prolapse

Goals of Women’s Health Physiotherapy

  • Reduce pain and discomfort
  • Restore pelvic floor function
  • Support safe exercise during and after pregnancy
  • Improve quality of life
  • Educate and empower women

Rehabilitation Strategies

  • Pelvic floor exercises
  • Manual therapy
  • Education on posture and ergonomics
  • Safe exercise prescription
  • Pain management techniques

Benefits of Physiotherapy

  • Reduced pain and symptoms
  • Improved pelvic health
  • Enhanced confidence and well-being

When to Seek Help

If you are experiencing pain, incontinence, or other women’s health concerns, consult a physiotherapist for a personalized assessment and care plan.
